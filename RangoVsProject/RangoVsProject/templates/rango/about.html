About Us!

TomaHack, founded by an electrical engineering student at Temple University, is a one stop shop for your business's needs. To cut to the chase, a representative talks to you about what kinds of utilities and software you would like to use for your small business or comapany. Then the representative talks pricing and delivery with the software team before following up with the client. Our company aims to use the currently popular software development methodology known as agile development with our own twists. Basically, we want to work with the client to get software that they will actually want . At times, software devs can have different visions than the client despite the initial planning phases and therefore at TomaHack, we want to bug you on a frequent basis about the progess of development and God Willing, get presentable software to yourself for demos during the project.
